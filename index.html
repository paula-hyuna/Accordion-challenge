Using Iceboxfun

Iceboxfun is your entertainment hub; a media-streaming, subscription-based service offering users access to over millions of movies, books, music, and game titles.

With Iceboxfun, you have access to our different media players: the Movie Player, Music Player, Books Reader, and the Game Launcher. All are easy to install and intuitive to use. For more info check our Technical Support section.

You can use Iceboxfun on any iOS, Android mobile device, or desktop.

As much as we'd love to provide all our users equal access to all the content hosted on Iceboxfun, the reality is that it's the content providers and rights holders that define the streaming rights of their content. In most cases, streaming rights are defined by territory so someone in another country may very well see a different content library.
